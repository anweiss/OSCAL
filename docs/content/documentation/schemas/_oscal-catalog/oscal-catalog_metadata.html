Publication metadata: metadata assembly

metadata is the root (containing) element of this schema.

Information describing the publication or availability of this document

The metadata assembly has the following contents (in order):

  • title field (one)
  • author fields (zero or more)
  • publication-date field (zero or one)
  • version field (zero or one)
  • doc-id fields (zero or more)
  • prop fields (zero or more)
  • hlink fields (zero or more)
  • resource assemblies (zero or more)
  • role assemblies (zero or more)
  • party assemblies (zero or more)
  • notes assembly (zero or one)
  • extra-meta assembly (zero or one)
