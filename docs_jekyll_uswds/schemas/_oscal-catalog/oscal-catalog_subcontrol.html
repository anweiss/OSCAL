Sub-Control: subcontrol assembly

A control extension or enhancement

The subcontrol assembly has the following contents (in order):

  • Flag id (required)
  • Flag class (optional)
  • A title field (one)
  • param assemblies (zero or more)
  • prop fields (zero or more)
  • link fields (zero or more)
  • part assemblies (zero or more)
  • A references assembly (zero or one)
