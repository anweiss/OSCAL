Section: section assembly

Allows the inclusion of prose content within a Catalog.

The section assembly has the following contents (in order):

  • Flag id (optional)
  • Flag class (optional)
  • A title field (one)
  • Prose contents (paragraphs and lists)
  • section assemblies (zero or more)
  • A references assembly (zero or one)
