Role: role assembly

Defining a role to be assigned to a party or agent

The role assembly has the following contents (in order):

  • Flag id (required)
  • A title field (zero or one)
  • A short-name field (zero or one)
  • A desc field (zero or one)

Permissible values to be determined closer to the application (e.g. by a receiving authority).
