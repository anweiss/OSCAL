Organization: org assembly

An organization or legal entity (not a person), with contact information

The org assembly has the following contents (in order):

  • org-name field (zero or one)
  • short-name field (zero or one)
  • org-id fields (zero or more)
  • address assembly (zero or one)
  • email fields (zero or more)
  • phone fields (zero or more)
  • url fields (zero or more)
  • notes assembly (zero or one)
